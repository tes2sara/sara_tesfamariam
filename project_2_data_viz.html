Supermarket Sales Vizualization.

My aim for this project is to analyze the Supermarket Sales Dataset to create a dashboard communicating some of the supermarket's key performance indicators. The interactive dashboard will display weekly key performance indicators to get valuable insights into the key sales and related figures, product types sold to track inventory turnover, customer satisfaction score, and segmentation of customers by membership, gender and payment method.

My initial adjustment is to change the date and time feature format to DateTime format and add a week number feature to make the weekly analysis straightforward. The subsequent step I took was to understand how many categories there are in the dataset.

After looking at the categories, I inserted a radio item that allows the user to choose whether the dashboard displays the combined branch results or the results of the individual branches separately.

Based on the chosen display the dashboard shows the reporting period, total sales, gross profit, gross profit percentage, cost of goods and average customer rating.

Moreover, the dashboard shows the weekly and daily sales trends for the chosen view. This allows users to get a snapshot of sales performance for a particular week or day of the week.

The dashboard also shows sales by product type to show the sales generated from the different product categories. Showing the contribution of products to the sales figure could help to curate products to match customers' demands.

Finally, to see the effect of members on sales, I have used the members and normal categories to drill further into the total payment they made, what kind of payment type they used, and their gender type.

For a closer look at the dashboard, please download the Jupyter Notebook and run it on your end.
